About

I am a research scientist at Joy Future Academy, where I explore multimodal foundation models, World Models, and AIGC. Prior to this, I was part of the Huawei TopMinds program (天才少年).

I earned my Ph.D. in the CSE Department at the Chinese University of Hong Kong in 2023, supervised by Prof. Jiaya Jia. During the doctoral study, my first-author work MAT was selected as a CVPR 2022 Best Paper Finalist. I received my Master's degree in 2019 and Bachelor's degree from the IEEE Pilot Class in 2016 from Shanghai Jiao Tong University.
